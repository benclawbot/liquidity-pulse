Scoring methodology

How Ideas Are Scored

Every idea is evaluated across four dimensions before it enters the board. No single dimension is sufficient — all four must clear their thresholds.

Theme Fit
Does the company benefit directly from a confirmed transmission chain or liquidity theme currently flagged on the dashboard? Passive sector exposure does not score.
Target: > 0.75
Regime Fit
Does the current liquidity regime — risk-on vs risk-off, expansion vs contraction — support this type of asset class and duration profile?
Target: > 0.65
Crowding
Positioning concentration score from prime brokerage flow data, options open interest, and ETF flows. High crowding = less edge per unit of risk.
Target: < 0.60
Entry Quality
Relative value vs historical trading range, vs sector, and vs fundamental成長. A strong idea with a poor entry is a watch-list name, not a portfolio idea.
Target: > 0.55
